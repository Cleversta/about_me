About me

I'm a self-taught maker who learned everything by building real things. No classroom — just curiosity, late nights, and a lot of trial and error.

I care about making products that feel simple and useful for everyday people. Whether it's helping someone remember a loved one's birthday, or track a warranty before it expires — I want what I build to actually matter to the people using it.

I focus on Southeast Asia — a region full of energy, diversity, and communities that deserve great products.
